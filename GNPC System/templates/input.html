About GNPC

The Ghana National Petroleum Corporation (GNPC) is the national oil company mandated to explore, develop, and manage Ghana’s petroleum resources for the benefit of the nation.

Our Operations

GNPC operates across the petroleum value chain, including upstream exploration, midstream processing, and downstream support activities, ensuring operational efficiency and national energy security.

Sustainability & ESG

Sustainability is central to GNPC’s mandate. The corporation integrates environmental protection, health and safety, and community development into all petroleum operations.
